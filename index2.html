About me

After 2020 I started a University project VillaDonq, where I took my first steps on Laravel, then in 2022 started SYSVER a project aimed to all "Gran Bases de Misiones de Paz" in Venezuela, usign Laravel for the API Back-end and React for the Front-end. I dedicated myself to improve my technique and skills to provide the best service

Projects

SISMED

Local inventory system designed specifically for the Health Secretary of Falcon state. Its primary goal is to enhance data visualization and ensure the integrity of information. By centralizing inventory data and implementing visual representation techniques, the system enables the Health Secretary to easily monitor stock levels and make informed decisions regarding resource allocation.

Work Experience

As a backend programmer, I achieved the proper balance of inventory inputs and outputs, as well as the correct visualization of inventory movements. Additionally, I implemented other features such as alerts for low stock and the display of general system statistics.

SYSVER

The local system aims to ensure the efficient management of administrative operations within the "Gran Base de Misiones de Paz Y Vida" in Falcon state. It is specifically designed to streamline and optimize the day-to-day administrative tasks carried out within the organization. By automating repetitive tasks and implementing standardized procedures, the system reduces manual effort, minimizes errors, and enhances overall operational efficiency.

Work Experience

SYSVER is a highly comprehensive project where I developed a module for managing user payments, account statuses, and debts.
