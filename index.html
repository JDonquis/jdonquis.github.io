About me

Hi! I'm a passionate web developer with more than 3 years of experience in creating web systems. In 2019 I started with Wordpress, where I build practice projects, usign tools like Oxigen, Elementor, and ACF

After 2020 I started a University project VillaDonq, where I took my first steps on Laravel, then in 2022 started SYSVER a project aimed to all "Gran Bases de Misiones de Paz" in Venezuela, usign Laravel for the API Back-end and React for the Front-end. In 2023, I started working as a developer for the Deparment of Health of Falcon, where I currently work on SISMED, a system for managing the inventories of all hospitals in the state

I dedicated myself to improve my technique and skills to provide the best service

Projects

SISMED

Local inventory system designed specifically for the Health Secretary of Falcon state. Its primary goal is to enhance data visualization and ensure the integrity of information. By centralizing inventory data and implementing visual representation techniques, the system enables the Health Secretary to easily monitor stock levels and make informed decisions regarding resource allocation.

Work Experience

As a backend programmer, I achieved the proper balance of inventory inputs and outputs, as well as the correct visualization of inventory movements. Additionally, I implemented other features such as alerts for low stock and the display of general system statistics.

The local system aims to ensure the efficient management of administrative operations within the "Gran Base de Misiones de Paz Y Vida" in Falcon state. By automating repetitive tasks and implementing standardized procedures, the system reduces manual effort, minimizes errors, and enhances overall operational efficiency.

Work Experience
